## Frequently asked questions

**Do I have to use Tally with friends?**
No. Tally works great on its own — track your habits, set goals, and build streaks solo. Friends are completely optional, for when you want a little accountability.

**How do I add a friend?**
Open the **Friends** tab, tap **Find by username**, search for your friend's username, and send a request. Once they accept, you'll see each other.

**How do I message a friend or start a group?**
Open a friend's profile and tap **Message**, or start a **group chat** from the Friends tab.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
In the app, open your **Profile** and tap **Manage subscription** — or go to iOS **Settings → your Apple Account → Subscriptions → Tally → Cancel**. Cancelling stops future renewals; it doesn't refund the current period (refunds are handled by Apple at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com)).

**How do I delete my account?**
From the **Today** tab, tap your profile photo in the top-right to open your **Profile**, then tap **Delete account** and confirm. This removes your data from iCloud. Note: deleting your account does **not** cancel your subscription — cancel that separately in Settings.

**A friend can't see me / something isn't syncing.**
Make sure both people are signed into iCloud with available storage, and that the app has been opened recently on both devices. If it keeps happening, email us.
